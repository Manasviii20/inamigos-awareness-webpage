About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). Based in Chhattisgarh and operating across India, the foundation works to create meaningful social impact through education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, community development and skill development initiatives.


Through dedicated volunteers, transparent operations and strategic partnerships, InAmigos Foundation continues to create positive change and improve lives across communities.

Our Projects

Project SEVA

Project SEVA supports underprivileged communities through food distribution, clothing support and welfare activities. The initiative has distributed more than 50,000 meals and continues to bring hope to those in need.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Project BACHPANSHALA helps underprivileged children through education support, digital literacy training and life skill development programs, creating opportunities for a brighter future.

Project JEEV

Project JEEV focuses on animal welfare by feeding and caring for stray animals while spreading awareness about kindness and responsible treatment of animals.

Project UDAAN

Project UDAAN empowers women through skill development, financial awareness, self-help group collaborations and menstrual hygiene awareness initiatives.

Project PRAKRITI

Project PRAKRITI promotes environmental conservation through plantation drives, sustainability awareness campaigns and eco-friendly initiatives. More than 20,000 saplings have been planted.

Project VIKAS

Project VIKAS focuses on skill development and career growth by providing internships and training opportunities across multiple domains. Over 30,000 interns have benefited.
